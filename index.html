Instructions
  • Press Enter Key to start the game & Run.
  • Press Space key to Jump.
  • Click Pause Button to pause the game
  • If you are hit by obstacle(bomb or flame), you will lost.
  • In level 1,if you collect 5 hearts you will get extra 200 points.
  • If you get 500 points as your score you will win level 1.
  • In level 2,if you collect 10 hearts you will get extra 400 points.
  • If you get 1000 points as your score you will win level 2.
